About me

I’m a builder at heart. For 15+ years I’ve lived in DevOps and release engineering—shipping things reliably, automating the boring parts, and keeping production calm. These days my focus is helping small teams adopt AI safely and cheaply: RAG over their own docs, lightweight guardrails, human-in-the-loop approvals, and simple dashboards so people actually trust what we deploy. I like projects that start small and prove value quickly. Most of my work happens in Python (FastAPI/Streamlit), Postgres/pgvector, and Docker, stitched together with IaC and CI/CD so it’s repeatable. When I’m not shipping something, I’m probably simplifying it.

DevOps for AI apps: a short checklist

  • Secrets: never in code; use env + secret manager.
  • Data paths: log what the model saw; tag PII; redact where needed.
  • Eval set: keep it small but real; run it on every change.
  • Rollouts: flags for model, prompt, and retriever; one click to roll back.
  • Observability: traces with latency, tokens, retrieval hits/misses, and feedback.
  • Cost: per-request budgets; alerts if you cross thresholds.

If you can’t see it and roll it back in minutes, it’s not ready.
